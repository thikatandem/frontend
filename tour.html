        What to expect

        A carefully planned, people-first experience

        Your visit begins with a welcome, tour briefing and introduction to the tandem bicycle. We explain how pilots and stokers communicate, how the club supports visually impaired athletes and how inclusive cycling builds independence, confidence and sporting opportunity.

        Depending on the chosen package, your experience may include tandem orientation, a guided local route, countryside riding, club stories, photography stops, refreshments and time with athletes, pilots, coaches or volunteers.

        Routes are selected according to traffic, road condition, weather, distance, group confidence and accessibility needs. Contact us early so we can design the best experience for your group.
