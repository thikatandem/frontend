        Privacy at Thika Tandem

        Clear, careful and respectful use of information

        This policy explains the information we may collect through our website, membership activities, events, volunteering, donations and communications.

        Information

        What we may collect

        Contact details, membership or participation information, safeguarding-relevant information, communications preferences and website form submissions.

        Purpose

        Why we use it

        To answer enquiries, manage club activities, protect participants, process support, improve services and send updates where consent has been given.

        Control

        Your choices

        You may ask to access, correct or delete information, or withdraw marketing consent, subject to legal and safeguarding requirements.

        Contact us
        Policy details

        How information is managed

        Lawful and fair use. We seek to use personal information only for clear club purposes and with an appropriate basis such as consent, legitimate club administration, safeguarding, contractual necessity or legal obligation.

        Sharing. We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with trusted service providers, event organisers, governing or safeguarding bodies, emergency services, or authorities when necessary and proportionate.

        Security and retention. We use reasonable organisational and technical measures and retain information only as long as needed for the purpose, record-keeping, safety or legal obligations.

        Children and vulnerable participants. Information connected with minors or safeguarding is handled with added care and restricted access.

        Cookies and external services. The site may use essential technical storage and third-party services such as maps, embedded media or analytics. Those providers may process information under their own policies.

        Updates. We may revise this policy when our activities or legal responsibilities change. The latest version published here applies.

        Privacy request

        Email us with “Privacy request” in the subject line. We may need to confirm your identity before releasing or changing personal information.
