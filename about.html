        Who we are

        A club built on trust, teamwork and possibility

        Thika Tandem ParaCycling Club is an inclusive cycling community based in Thika, Kenya. We bring visually impaired athletes and sighted pilots together on tandem bicycles so they can train, compete, grow in confidence and experience the freedom of cycling.

        Our mission

        Open the road to opportunity

        We create safe, structured and ambitious pathways for visually impaired athletes to participate in cycling, develop their talent and pursue competitive goals.

        Our vision

        An inclusive cycling future

        We envision a Kenya where disability never limits access to sport, community, leadership or the chance to perform at the highest level.

        Our approach

        Two riders, one team

        Every tandem partnership depends on communication, discipline and mutual trust—qualities that shape our training, our culture and our impact beyond cycling.

        What we do

        From first ride to race day

        Our work combines athlete development, tandem pilot partnerships, regular training, competition preparation and community engagement. We help riders build fitness, technical skill, confidence and a strong sense of belonging.

        We also work with volunteers, coaches, supporters and partners who believe inclusive sport can change lives and strengthen communities.

        What guides us

        Our values on and off the bike

        Inclusion

        Everyone deserves a place in sport

        We remove barriers, respect individual needs and design opportunities around the person—not the disability.

        Excellence

        Progress with purpose

        We train with discipline, learn continuously and support every athlete to reach their strongest possible level.

        Teamwork

        Trust moves us forward

        Riders, pilots, coaches, families and supporters succeed when communication and shared responsibility come first.

        Our community

        Rooted in Thika, riding for Kenya

        Our home is Thika in Kiambu County, where local roads, training sessions and community relationships shape the club. From this base, we aim to grow para-cycling participation, represent our community with pride and connect Kenyan athletes to wider opportunities.

        The club

        What is tandem para-cycling?

        A tandem bicycle is ridden by two people: a sighted pilot at the front and a visually impaired athlete, often called a stoker, at the rear. Both riders pedal and compete as one team.

        Participation

        Who can join or support?

        Visually impaired athletes, potential pilots, coaches, volunteers, partners and supporters are welcome to contact the club.

        Support

        How can organisations help?

        Partners can support equipment, training, travel, athlete welfare, events, visibility and long-term programme growth.
